How to Play

  1. Tap any cell to start. Your goal is to fill as many cells on the grid as possible by placing numbers in sequence.
  2. Move by jumping cells. You can move in two ways:
    • Cardinal Jump: Skip 2 cells horizontally or vertically
    • Diagonal Jump: Skip 1 cell diagonally
  3. Plan your path carefully. You can't revisit cells you've already filled. One wrong move and you might get stuck!
  4. Earn stars based on how much of the grid you fill: 80% = 1 star, 90% = 2 stars, 100% = 3 stars (Perfect!).
  5. Progress through the collection. Complete levels to unlock the next. Finish 10 levels in a stage to unlock the next stage.
